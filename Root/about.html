Roller Skateshop Company Profile

The Roller Story

Roller Skateshop is a young and dynamic organisation that was founded in 2017, by two entrepreneurs "Sam" and "Kevin". The company started out with a single store in Amsterdam. We both had the vision to building a platform that enables people to explore urban or wildlife. We share a dream that people can hop on a board at any time, whether if its in the office or at your cousin's wedding.
